Estimate Terms for Large-Scale Projects

  • For larger works, we provide a segmented proposal with clear phases (planning, mobilization, execution, handover).
  • After the site visit and scope confirmation, a down payment of up to 40% is required to reserve dates and mobilize the crew.
  • Once the project starts, subsequent payments follow the agreed schedule to enable material purchases and continuous progress.
  • The payment schedule will be presented in the proposal/contract with milestones, dates, and conditions.

Process — How We Work

End‑to‑end transparency for accurate estimates and efficient delivery.

1

Share your project

Send photos, description, dimensions or floor plan — or schedule a site visit.

2

On‑site assessment

We take measurements and validate constraints to ensure an accurate estimate.

3

Detailed proposal

We present phases, timelines, pricing, conditions, and a payment schedule.

4

Approval + deposit

Upon approval, a deposit (up to 40%) reserves the schedule and mobilizes the team.

5

Mobilization

Materials purchasing, logistics, and crew allocation.

6

Execution

Work proceeds per schedule and best practices for safety and quality.

7

Oversight

Supervision, inspections, and any adjustments aligned with the client.

8

Handover

Final delivery with punch‑list fixes as applicable.

Tip

The more info (photos, dimensions, references), the faster and more accurate the estimate.
